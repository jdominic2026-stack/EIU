About Equatoria International University

Our Story

Founded in June 2019 in Torit, South Sudan, Equatoria International University has been at the forefront of providing quality education and professional development opportunities. We serve students and professionals across diverse fields including public health, business management, computer science, and community education.

Our Commitment to SDG 4

We are deeply committed to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4. Through our comprehensive educational programs, we ensure access to quality education, promote digital literacy, support skills development, and empower communities to achieve their full potential.

What We Provide

Beyond traditional courses, we offer learning materials including notes, tutorials, videos, past papers, and assignments. We actively promote digital literacy through computer and smartphone training, information research skills, and digital communication techniques.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create a well-educated, skilled, and empowered society where every citizen has equal access to quality education and opportunities for a better, more sustainable future.

  • Transform education through innovation
  • Build inclusive learning communities
  • Develop competent, ethical professionals
  • Contribute to global development

Our Mission

We ensure access to education for all, improve literacy and skills, promote peace and national unity, empower youth and women, support economic development, improve health and social awareness, and promote lifelong learning across communities.

  • Provide equitable quality education
  • Foster digital transformation
  • Support community empowerment
  • Align with global standards
